Gulab Jamun

Ingridents

  • Sugar
  • Bread
  • Oil

Recipe

  1. Prepare the dough: Combine milk solids (khoya) or milk powder with a little flour and baking soda to form a soft dough. Gently roll the dough into smooth, crack-free balls.
  2. Make the sugar syrup: Boil sugar and water until the sugar dissolves. Add cardamom pods or powder and rose water for flavor, then set aside to keep it warm.
  3. Fry the jamuns: Deep-fry the dough balls in oil or ghee over a low flame until they turn a uniform golden brown. Remove them and let them cool for a few minutes.
  4. Soak and serve: Immediately add the fried jamuns into the warm sugar syrup. Let them soak for at least 30 minutes to two hours, allowing them to expand and absorb the syrup before serving.

Barfi Jamun

Ingredients

  • Khoya or Milk Powder
  • Flour and a pinch of Baking Soda
  • Milk or Water
  • Ghee or Oil (for frying)
  • Sugar and Water (for syrup)
  • Cardamom or Rose Water

Recipe

  1. Prepare Dough: Mix milk solids, flour, and baking soda. Knead with a little milk to form a soft dough, then roll into small, smooth balls.
  2. Make Syrup: Boil sugar and water. Flavor with cardamom or rose water. Keep the syrup warm.
  3. Fry Jamuns: Deep-fry the balls slowly in oil or ghee over low heat until they are deep golden brown.
  4. Soak and Serve: Immediately transfer the fried balls into the warm syrup. Let them soak for at least 30 minutes before serving.
