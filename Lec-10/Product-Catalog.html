Books

A book catalog is an organized list of all the bibliographic items in a collection, such as a personal library. Its primary purpose is to help users find and locate a book by providing multiple access points, such as author, title, and subject. Whether a simple list or a complex digital database, a good catalog is a key to navigating any book collection.
